About Ladies Who Farm Platform

LADIES WHO FARM PLATFORM

Introduction

Ladies who farm is a woman only, a safe space for women to grow each other through sharing their journey, pitfalls, and experiences. Above all it’s a platform to see agriculture with the lenses of a woman and adjust policies through advocacy to include women farmers in the table. It is also a marketing place and offers peer to peer knowledge.

Women play a critical role in the future of food security. There is an increasing interest and participation of women in agricultural activities as trends toward improved efficiency and environmental sustainability continue. Women also provide the effects by working together around the table, rather than apart from each other, in their respective communities and making decisions for better opportunities for themselves, their families and others.

The overall goal of Ladies who Farm Platform is to enhance economic, social, and environmental livelihoods of women farmers in Africa through increased adoption of ecologically sustainable agriculture. More specifically, our gender sensitive interventions aim to

  • 1. Improve working conditions of women in the farms.
  • 2. Financial literacy and agribusiness management
  • 3. Incorporate Agriculture and Technology
  • 4. Increase smallholder farmers’ incomes, improved productivity
  • 5. Enhanced households’ food resilience.

    • These areas align with the country’s Agricultural Sector Transformation and Growth Strategy priority focus areas – increase small-scale farmer, pastoralist and fisherfolk incomes; increase agricultural output and value addition; and, boost household food resilience. The fight against hunger and poverty is bound closely with global challenges of climate change, loss of biodiversity and inequality.
